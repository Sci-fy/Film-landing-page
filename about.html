ABOUT OBSTACLE

Emotionally intelligent brand storytelling through film since 2012.

OUR STORY

Obstacle Films brings emotional intelligence to brand storytelling through people-centric documentaries and films.

Founded in 2012, we've grown into a respected film production studio known for our authentic approach to storytelling and our ability to connect with audiences on a human level.

Our work spans commercials, music videos, documentary films, and branded content, always with a focus on creating meaningful connections between brands and their audiences.
