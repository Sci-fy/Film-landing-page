OUR STORY

Obstacle Films brings emotional intelligence to brand storytelling through people-centric documentaries and films. Since 2012.

Obstacle Films is a film production studio led by BIFA-nominated filmmaker Oliver Nias, Creative Partner and filmmaker Matt Roberts, Executive Producer Hugo Peers (MJZ, Sonny London, Independent Entertainment) and Producer Erin Hughes.

Our expert people, fine-tuned services and caring approach means that investment in storytelling with Obstacle guarantees brands an emotional connection with their audience.

Obstacle Films operates globally and has extensive international production experience. We have produced films on every continent except for Antarctica.
